industry and Healthcare Executive Administrator Roundtable Sessions

What Participants Can Expect

Join your colleagues for cutting-edge knowledge sharing with established thought leaders and subject matter experts, informed perspectives from research authorities, and best practice presentations from hands-on front-line innovators and implementers of change.

View Agenda

Together, we will explore the essential strategic and operational imperatives for the healthcare executive administrator audience. We will illuminate the road ahead from an evolving healthcare reform policy perspective, examine the current landscape, and share the experiences of hospitals and health systems actively engaged in overcoming today's challenges.
